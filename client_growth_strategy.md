## 4. The Growth Narrative: Why Brian + phData = Accelerated Growth

### The Core Thesis

phData has built an exceptional reputation in **data platform modernization**---particularly
around Snowflake, dbt, and cloud migration. Their case study portfolio demonstrates deep
expertise in transforming enterprise data infrastructure. However, the market is rapidly
shifting: clients increasingly want **AI/ML outcomes**, not just data platforms. They want
the platform AND the intelligence layer on top.

Brian Cohn fills this exact gap. He brings:

### 4.1 Credibility That Opens Doors

When Brian walks into a pharmaceutical company, he doesn't just talk about Snowflake
schemas---he talks about clinical trial data management, FDA compliance, and how GenAI
can accelerate drug discovery. He has an NIH grant, a Nature publication, and real
clinical experience. This credibility is impossible to hire for and invaluable for
winning healthcare and life sciences deals.

### 4.2 The AstraZeneca Door-Opener

Brian's direct experience building the AIMI virtual assistant for AstraZeneca is a
concrete proof point. AstraZeneca and its peers (Pfizer, Merck, Novartis, etc.) are
all investing billions in data and AI. Brian's relationship with AstraZeneca could
serve as a beachhead for phData to enter the top-20 pharmaceutical companies---a
market where a single engagement can be worth $5-20M+.

### 4.3 The GenAI Premium

phData's portfolio shows growing AI/ML work, but most case studies still center on
data platform modernization. Brian's GenAI expertise (production LLM systems, RAG
architectures, agentic AI) lets phData command premium rates for AI-forward projects.
GenAI engagements typically carry 30-50% higher rates than platform work, and Brian
can both sell and deliver these projects.

### 4.4 The Medical Device & Health Tech Vertical

phData has some healthcare case studies but no dedicated medical device practice.
Brian's experience as a medical device co-founder, clinical trial PI, and healthcare
AI developer means he could build this vertical from scratch. The medical device
market's data needs are exploding due to FDA's increasing expectations for real-world
evidence and AI/ML-enabled devices.

### 4.5 The Consulting Network Multiplier

As Sr. Architect at Credera (part of Omnicom Group), Brian has visibility into a vast
network of enterprise clients. While Credera focuses on management and digital
consulting, phData specializes in data platform implementation. This creates a natural
referral pipeline: Credera identifies client data challenges -> phData delivers the
technical solution. Brian is the bridge that makes this partnership work.
